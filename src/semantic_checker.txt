Conditions that could be checked by an eventual semantic checker:
- input shall be initialized
- input can never be modified a part for initialization
- ouptut shall be initialized
- output can never be used in transitions condition
- [WARN] transitions shall not be order-dependent
- there shall not be undefined behaviors among transitions (all combinations of conditions shall be convered by one and only one transition)
- there shall not be two or more transitions with the same condition
- transition condition can contain only input and local variables
- [WARN] there shall not be loops without a 'retry'/'timeout' guard
- 'retry' condition shall contain only constants
